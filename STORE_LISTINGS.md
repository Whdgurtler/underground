## FAQ for Store Listing

**Q: Does this app work outside Toronto?**
A: Yes! While designed for Toronto's PATH, it works anywhere GPS signals are blocked.

**Q: Does it drain battery?**
A: The app uses GPS and sensors which do consume battery, but it's optimized for efficiency.

**Q: Is my location tracked?**
A: No! All data stays on your device. We don't collect or share any location data.

**Q: Do I need internet?**
A: Internet is needed to download map tiles, but the core positioning features work offline.

**Q: How accurate is underground positioning?**
A: Accuracy varies but is generally good for 1-2 minutes. CalibRate regularly for best results.

**Q: Is this really free?**
A: Yes! No ads, no in-app purchases, completely free.
